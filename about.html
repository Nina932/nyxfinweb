Our Approach

Technology Philosophy

"LLMs explain, never compute." — The core architectural principle of NYX Core.

Symbolic Grounding

We anchor creative AI reasoning in hard mathematical constraints. The Symbolic Brain produces the financial truth using Python Decimal precision; the Neural Brain interprets that truth. AI results that violate accounting invariants are automatically discarded.

Pragmatic Intelligence

We anchor every insight in the cold reality of your accounting records. By automating the reconciliation of trial balances and transaction logs, we ensure that every report and alert is grounded in verifiable truth.

The Analyst, Not the Dashboard

Dashboards tell you what happened. NYX Core identifies why. By automatically decomposing your financial waterfall, we act as the high-level financial analyst that can scale with your business.
