We're democratizing financial intelligence for mid-market companies in emerging economies. Today, institutional-grade financial tools like Palantir and Anaplan are priced for Fortune 500 enterprises, leaving mid-market businesses relying on spreadsheets and outdated systems.

NYX Core bridges this gap with deterministic AI that's affordable, offline-capable, and locally compliant. We believe financial intelligence is a right, not a luxury.

Our Approach

Technology Philosophy

Deterministic First

Financial math must be precise. Every calculation uses Python Decimal with ROUND_HALF_UP. Zero LLM dependency in core computations.

Multi-Agent Architecture

Real agents with independent health tracking, circuit breakers, and tool routing. Not wrappers. Not demos. Purpose-built for financial analysis.

Knowledge-Driven

700+ entities encoding Georgian accounting domain knowledge. The system understands the business, not just the numbers.

Resilient by Design

5-tier LLM fallback, offline capability, deterministic templates when AI is unavailable. Always operational.
