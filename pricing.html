Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work? ▼
Our free trial gives you full access to your chosen plan for 14 days. No credit card required to start. You'll have access to all features including report generation, anomaly detection, and API endpoints. At the end of your trial, you can subscribe to continue or your account will be archived.
Can I switch plans anytime? ▼
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing based on your current cycle. If you upgrade mid-month, you'll only pay the difference. Downgrades apply to your next billing period.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and wire transfers. Enterprise customers can arrange custom payment terms and annual billing options. All payments are processed securely through industry-standard payment gateways.
Is my financial data secure? ▼
Yes. We use bank-level encryption, SOC 2 Type II compliance certification, and regular third-party security audits. All data is encrypted both at rest and in transit using AES-256 encryption. Enterprise customers can deploy on-premise for complete data control and compliance with local regulations.
Do you offer discounts for annual billing? ▼
Yes. Annual billing plans receive a 20% discount compared to monthly pricing. For Professional tier, that's $1,790/year instead of $2,232. Enterprise customers can negotiate custom pricing based on their usage, commitment length, and specific requirements.
What's the difference between cloud and on-premise? ▼
Cloud deployment is our standard offering—fully managed, automatically updated, and accessible from anywhere. On-premise is available for Enterprise customers and runs entirely on your infrastructure, giving you complete control over data, compliance, and customization. On-premise deployments require dedicated infrastructure and support.
